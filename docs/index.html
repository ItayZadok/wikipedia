Ofek Rozenfeld, also known as "1uhvofek" (born 4 December 2012) is a Jewish, Israeli cutie patuti, programmer, witch, spiker, biologist and ice skater. Ofek is best known as the most popular CS:GO Twitch streamer of all time. Since 2025, Ofek has joined the Spikes 2212# FRC group, making several advances in fields such as writing the best autonomous ever and teaching hamshushim how to ooga booga.

Early life

[edit]

Ofek Rozenfeld was born on 4 December 2012 to Eli Rozenfeld, a programmer and Moran Rozenfeld, a manager at Bank Hapoalim, "highschool lovebirds" (as Ofek exposed in a later interview). Ofek has a sister named Agam, which is an incredible baker (@see - the best cookies ever), that hates "loud noises" and "you got an amplifier for your guitar?! Stop making so much noise".

Ofek attended Ben Shemen Youth Village elementary school where she started her witchcraft expertise. She then attended Nachshon middle school, where she began activating her forbidden magic, on several subjects such as "Noam Tekes" and becoming a full-on kawai UwU anime girl. Then, she attended Atid "Madaim" Lod high school, overdosed on Advil and died. RIP.
